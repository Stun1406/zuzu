WHAT IS TALENT OLIVE?

Talent Olive is a guided career exploration platform designed to help students discover possible career pathways based on their interests, subject inclinations, learning preferences, and emerging opportunities.

1. STRUCTURED CAREER EXPLORATION

Talent Olive is a structured Career Exploration Tool (CET) designed to help students reflect on their interests, subject inclinations, learning preferences, and emerging career possibilities.

2. FOCUSES ON GUIDED EXPLORATION

Unlike conventional psychometric tests that attempt to categorize students into fixed personality or aptitude types, TO-CET focuses on guided exploration of interests, subjects, emerging opportunities, and possible career pathways.

3. PILOTED & REVIEWED

The framework was piloted with students from grades 8–12 and reviewed by parents, educators, and psychology graduates for relevance, clarity, and practical usefulness.

4. IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER

TO-CET is a career exploration and guidance framework and should not be treated as a psychological or clinical assessment.
